FAQ

Why do I need Joomba?

Joomba is a tenant-rewards platform that shifts your relationship with tenants from a transaction-based perspective to hospitality-focused. Through our platform, your renters get an extra incentive to pay rent on time, stay longer and remain engaged in their rent experience.

How do my tenants pay their rent?

Joomba was built with flexibility in mind. If you don't want your tenants to pay their rent through our platform they don't have to! You can use whatever method you've been using and we will work with you to verify each payment.

How does Joomba make money?

Joomba has exclusive partnerships with brands and businesses on the platform that offer revenue generating opportunities for the business. This allows Joombaa to offer our revolutionary product at very low cost to our landlords!

How is this any different from a Property Management Company

Property Managers typically charge you some money and deal with tenants on your behalf. This works most of the time, but it often comes at the expense of the relationship with your tenants. However,when you use Joomba, we eliminate the need for third parties and let your tenants share in your property's success. Instead of paying a stranger to harrass our tenants , why not share it with your tenant? When your tenants win, you win!
