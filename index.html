FAQ

Why do I need Joomba?

Joomba is a tenant-rewards platform that shifts your relationship with tenants from transaction-based to hospitality-focused. Through our platform, your renters get an extra incentive to pay rent on time and stay engaged in their rent experience.

I don't need a new Property MAnagement SOlution. What I've got worksQuestion 2

Joomba isn't a property management platform we do one thing and one thing only: maximise your tenant engagement. We don't require your tenants to pay rent through our platform, neither do we require you to allow us to manage your property. We are a standalone rewards platform for your renters. You can continue paying rent through your current system.
